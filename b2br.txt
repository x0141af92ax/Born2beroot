diff between centos and Debian?
Debian is a distro of Linux, stable, used in servers a lot, getting less updates, uses apt, suitable for people who are new to Linux world.
CentOs is a distro from red hat enterprise Linux, stable, used in companies, more updates than Linux especially in security, uses YUM, suitable for companies.
